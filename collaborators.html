Nsai Frankline Sanyuy
Songhai Labs
Nsai Frankline Sanyuy is a Junior Public Health Epidemiologist with a focus on delivering maximum and satisfactory services in the field of public health with specialization in Infectious Disease Epidemiology. As a professional epidemiologist with interest in data analysis and management, Frankline has been a figure head in a number of projects, both implementation and research with a record publication and hopes to publish more. He has been an instructor at the Catholic School of Health Sciences, Shisong, in the northwest region of Cameroon where he took the lead role in academic and research activities and has since then contributed immensely in improving the quality of research in the said institution. For the past two years, Frankline has also been involved in health implementation activities under the Ministry of Health in the Kumbo West Health District where he acts as a mentor and a field supervisor. He has seen progress in his academic life, where by after obtaining an honors Degree in Microbiology (BSc) from the Faculty of Science of the University of Buea in 2011, he went on with his postgraduate studies in Epidemiology and Control of Infectious Diseases where he graduated in 2014 still from the same institution. Currently, he is doing his PhD. in Public Health at the Faculty of Health Sciences where he is developing more experience in grant proposal writing and project management.
