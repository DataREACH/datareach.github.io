Mission Statement

As data science continues to grow as an integral part of life in the 21st century, its applications around the world continue to diversify and change the status quo. Our mission is to harness the powerful applications of machine learning and predictive analytics to make a positive impact on medicine all around the world. Through building powerful software applications connecting cutting edge advances in machine learning/artificial intelligence and molecular biology we hope to improve patient outcomes on a global scale.
